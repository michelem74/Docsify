# What is DEVONthink?

Every day you have to deal with *immense amounts of information*: texts, PDFs, websites, images, emails, spreadsheets. You work with them at school, in the editorial office, in the law firm, in the medical practice, and in many other places. DEVONthink stores this information as documents in *easy-to-backup databases* where you structure, organize, and work with them. Even [revision-proof](https://www.devontechnologies.com/apps/devonthink/security#revisionproof), if needed.
